# Privacy Policy — Mercedes App

**Last updated:** 2026-04-18

## 1. Who We Are

Mercedes App ("we", "us", "our") operates the Mercedes training management application. For questions about this policy, contact us at adkeswani@gmail.com.

## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Data (via Google Sign-In)

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store:

- **Display name** — your Google account name
- **Email address** — your Google account email
- **Profile photo URL** — your Google profile picture link
- **Firebase UID** — a unique identifier assigned by Firebase Authentication

### 2.2 Profile Data

After signing in, you may provide:

- **Username** — a unique handle you choose during onboarding
- **Discoverability preference** — whether other users can find you by username

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Data

- **Firebase Authentication tokens** — managed by Google Firebase for session management
- **Firestore document timestamps** — when your data was created or last modified

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

- Authenticate you and maintain your session
- Create and manage your user profile
- Enable app features (training programs, workout tracking, messaging — as they become available)
- Enforce access controls (e.g., ensuring only program owners can manage their programs)

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in **Google Firebase** (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data may be stored in the United States or other regions where Google Cloud operates. See [Google Cloud's data processing terms](https://cloud.google.com/terms/data-processing-addendum) for details.

## 5. Data Retention

- Your account data is retained as long as your account is active.
- If you delete your account, we initiate a cascading cleanup that removes or anonymizes your data across all collections (user profile, workout data, messages, etc.).
- Some anonymized or aggregated data may be retained for operational purposes.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — view your profile and associated data within the app
- **Correct** your data — update your profile information at any time
- **Delete** your data — delete your account, which triggers removal of your personal data
- **Export** your data — request a data export (available when the export feature is implemented)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at adkeswani@gmail.com.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Mercedes App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us at adkeswani@gmail.com.
